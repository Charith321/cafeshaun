Café Shaun

Café Shaun Kandy is one of the best coffee houses in Sri Lanka. Café Shaun is well known for emphasizing on serving quality premium coffee all the time.

Café Shaun is a startup which was created to cater to the true authentic coffee shop experience, by emphasizing on high-quality premium coffee

Serving the World's Best Coffee

We believe coffee should be as simple or complex as you want it to be. From the origin topography, to the water quality used in the end pour and everything in between, we obsess on every possible variable. Our ambition is to serve great tasting coffee from the best beans we can source each season from around the world.

A Little Bit Of Holiday In Every Cup

The best way to get into the holiday spirit? Coffee that warms your heart. Enjoy the return of holiday favorites Cookie Butter, Brewed Coffee, Espresso, Cappuccino, Ice Blended Drinks, Mocha.
